Must Watch Movies

From Dark to Mystery, Crime and Action. Great movies from the 90s to date

The Pianist(History, War, Drama)

I watched this movie after briefly reading The Gulag Archipelago by Alexander Solzhenitsyn. The horrors were more vivid in my psych. The trials and torment of the Jews in Poland during the WWII.
Directed By Roman Polanski.

Schindlers-list(War, History, Drama)

Just like the Pianist, this movie is about the Jews in Poland. This is a sad movie about a german industrialist and his relationship with his jew workers.Its not for the faint of heart.
Directed by Steven Spielberg.

Her(Romance/Sci-fi)

Her is a movie about the lonely Theodore (played by the infamous Joaquin Phoenix) who develops an unusual relationship with an AI(Scarlett Johansson). This is a movie for the loners
Directed by Spike Jonze

Little Miss Sunshine(Comedy,Drama,Adventure)
